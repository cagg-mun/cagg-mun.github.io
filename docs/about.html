About¶

The Computational Applied Geophysics Group at Memorial University is a multi-disciplinary research group whose goals are to develop numerical modelling and high-performance computing techniques for all fields of applied geophysics.

Please see http://www.esd.mun.ca/~farq/cagg.html for more information.

A selection of current projects can be found at http://www.esd.mun.ca/~farq/cagg_research.html.

A sister project, the Atlantic Collaborative Research Group in Numerical Analysis and Scientific Computing, can be found http://www.math.mun.ca/anasc/.
